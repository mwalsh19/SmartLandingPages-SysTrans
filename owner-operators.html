To join Swift as an Owner Operator, you must meet the below requirements:

Truck Insurance
  • Physical damage insurance—available at 2.5% per $1,000.00 of truck value.
  • Non-trucking liability insurance—available through Swift at $5.77 per week.
  • Occupational Accident per Hazard insurance—available at $32.31 or $45.00 per week.
  • Insurance is available through our preferred vendor or you have the option of using your own insurance. Your insurance policy must comply with our contract requirements.
Eligibility Requirements for an Owner Operator not Employed by Swift
  • Must be at least 21 years old.
Tractor Requirements
  • Your tractor must pass a DOT 1 level inspection
  • 3-axle with sleeper and sliding fifth wheel, and preferably low-profile tires. Maximum (WB 265) wheelbase; maximum weight- 22,000 lbs. with fuel
